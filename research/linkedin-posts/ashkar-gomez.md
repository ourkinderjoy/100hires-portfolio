## Post 1
Topic: Why most B2B SaaS brands are invisible in AI search results

Key insight: LLMs retrieve content based on entity trust signals, not just backlinks. If your brand isn't consistently mentioned across trusted third-party sources, AI systems won't include you in generated answers — no matter how good your website is.

## Post 2
Topic: Entity mapping is the new keyword research

Key insight: In traditional SEO you map keywords. In AI SEO you map entities — your brand, your category, your competitors, and the relationships between them. The goal is to make your brand the most clearly defined entity in your space.

## Post 3
Topic: How to get cited by AI systems — a practical framework

Key insight: Three things drive AI citation: being mentioned by authoritative sources, having structured and clearly attributed content, and maintaining consistent brand signals across the web. This is controllable and measurable.
