## Post 2
**Topic:** Why most B2B SaaS companies are doing SEO backwards

Key insight: Most companies start with keywords and work backwards to content. The right approach is to start with the customer's pain points and work forwards to the keywords. Pain Point SEO always beats keyword-first SEO.
